Avatar Component

The Avatar component is used to represent a user, and displays the profile picture, initials or fallback icon.

Usage

Avatars in their simplest form display content, by default, within a circular container.



Sizes

Want more control over your avatars? Add ".avatar-lg" , ".avatar-md" or ".avatar-sm" classes for additional sizes.



Shapes

Want some other different shapes for your Avatars? Add ".avatar-rounded-corner" or ".avatar-square" classes for some additional shapes. Default shape is circle, so no extra classes needed for that.
